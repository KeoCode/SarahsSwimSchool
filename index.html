Welcome to Sarah's Swim School

Here at Sarah's Swim School we pride ourselves on providing the best service for our clients, through hiring highly qualified swim instructors and supplying all the equipment and tools required to give you the best.
We provide swimming lessons for everyone and every reason, if you are looking for lessons for your child or looking to learn this life saving skill yourself.

We offer lessons to cater for everyone and every need.

About us

Who we are

Sarah is a fully qualified swim teacher with over 10 years experience teaching swim lessons. She has dreamed of providing quality lessons with the best team.

All our team are highly qualified to the highest in their standard in their field.

All our instructors are Garda vetted, are qualified Swim teachers as well as having extra training in toddler swim teaching, long distance swim techniques and qualified lifegaurds.

You can be assured that there is no one better qualified and no person you can trust more in the water.

What we do

Private lessons

One on one - private teaching which is ideal for some one who feels they would learn better with the instructors full attention, be it how you learn best or if its due to a disablity.
Two on one - if you and someone you know feel more comfortable learning together and are both around the same level, we can accommodate two people with one instructor.

Group Lessons

Swim Tots - For parent and baby lessons. Ideal for babies from 6 months to 4 years old, to ensure they are comfortable and water loving babies
Children's Lessons - Providing lessons for all levels, 5 days a week, on both evenings and weekends, for any child from 4- 14 years old.
Adults Group lessons - Providing lessons for all levels from beginner to advanced lane swimming. wheather you are taking your first dip in the pool or perfecting technique for your first long distance swim - we've got the teacher for you!
